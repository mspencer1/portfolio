About Me

I am a graphic designer based in Hamilton working mainly on web design, packaging and print production. Passionately curious creative, artistic, innovative, independent, good in group projects and strong in leading positions. Always thinking outside the box.

I have an affection for all things design related and love to see a finished product that I had a hand in creating. Making creative and new designs is one of my favourite things to do. I strive to constantly enhance my design skills and I’m always ready to learn something new. With the experience gained through my time in the industry, I have been given the opportunity to understand and execute the many design processes, including web design, print production, and package design with great proficiency.
