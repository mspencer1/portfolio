About Me

I'm a digital designer with a background in web, graphic design, and mechanical engineering. Currently, I am working in the field as a Web Designer at Printing By Innovation Inc. I'm interested in working with modern web technologies, making creative and new designs, and enhancing my web and design skills.

Having studied both design & illustration as well as mechanical engineering, my perspective is at once creative and analytical. I'm a meticulous and dynamic person and always take the time and effort to fully understand the challenges I'm presented with. I have a particular ability to work things out independently but also thrive in team environments of motivated individuals.

I've worked with companies of all shapes and sizes, so contact me if you'd like to work together on your next project.
